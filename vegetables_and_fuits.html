FRUITS

A fruit is a developed ovary of a plant that comes from a flower and contains one or more multiple seeds.

fruits are categorized into two:

  • Fleshy fruits
  • Dry fruits

Fleshy fruits

They comprise a fleshy outer layer within a leathery skin.They include:

  • Blueberry
  • Cucumber
  • Mulberry
  • Brinjal

dry fruits

They are fruits that have been dried either naturally or artificially.they include:

  1. raisins
  2. plums
  3. figs
  4. apricots

VEGETABLES

Are parts of plants that are consumed by humans or other animals as food.

Types of vegetables

  • Leafy green-spinach
  • Cruciferous-cabbage
  • Marrow-pumpkin
  • Allium-onion,garlic
  • Edible plant stem-celery
  • Roots-sweet potato and yams

Importance of fruits and vegetables

fruits and vegetables are essential for a healthy diet.They provide the body with the necessary needs to build itself and they are a good source of fiber, vitamins and minerals
